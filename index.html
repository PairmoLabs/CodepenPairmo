⭐ User Rating System

Each user has a visible rating score (e.g., 4.7) based on community feedback.
You can rate others after each completed interaction – whether it’s a travel buddy, a client, or a conversation.

Higher scores increase visibility and trust on the platform.

🏅 Dynamic Badges

Badges are automatically unlocked based on your behavior, preferences, and activity.
They’re fully dynamic — they appear, disappear, and evolve as you use the app.

  • 🌆 CityBreak – You love short urban trips
  • ✅ SafeMeet – ID & selfie verified
  • 🚀 ActiveVibe – Interactions in the last 3 days
  • 📅 LastMinuteReady – Your trip starts in less than 3 days
  • 🧩 GroupPlanner – You created a crew plan

You don’t apply for badges — you earn them.
